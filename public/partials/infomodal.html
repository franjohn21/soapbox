Welcome to

Here's how it works:

1. Soapbox uses your location to find others in your area.
2. You can send splashes to people within range (1000 meters) at the time the splash was sent. You will receive splashes from those around you. If this is your first time logging in, the most recent splashes made will be added to your list, regardless of location
3. As those around you send splashes, you will receive them if you are in range. You can then comment on the splashes that were sent to you.
4. You can track where you've been and what people have been talking about at different locations throughout your day.
